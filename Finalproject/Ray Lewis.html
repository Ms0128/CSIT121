Ray Lewis

The Undisputed Defensive Heavyweight Champion

Ray Lewis was a legendary linebacker who played for the Baltimore Ravens from 1996 to 2012. He was one of the greatest defensive players of all time and was known for his intensity, leadership, and work ethic. He was a 13-time Pro Bowler and a 7-time First-Team All-Pro. He won two Defensive Player of the Year awards and was a key player in the Ravens' two Super Bowl victories. Off the field, Lewis was a respected leader and mentor, both in the locker room and in the community. He was known for his charitable work and his commitment to helping others. Ray Lewis has had many notable football achievements throughout his career. He was a 13-time Pro Bowler, a 7-time First-Team All-Pro, and a 2-time Defensive Player of the Year. He was also a 2-time Super Bowl champion and was named the MVP of Super Bowl XXXV.Ray Lewis holds several records in the NFL. He is the Ravens' all-time leader in tackles, sacks, and interceptions.
