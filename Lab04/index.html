Bodacious Burrito Recipe

Bodacious Ingredients

  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 Bodacious tortillas
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup cilantro
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1 cup of BODACIOUS sauce

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and bell pepper, cook until softened, usually about 5 minutes.
  3. Add black beans, chili powder, cumin, garlic poweder, parika, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 5-8 minutes.
  4. Warm tortillas in microwave or on stove.
  5. Divide theblack bean mixture evenly among tortillas.
  6. Top with sliced avocado, cilantro, and slasa.
  7. Roll up burritos and serve.
